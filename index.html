Intro

Hey there! I'm a passionate Software Developer with a knack for crafting digital experiences that leave a lasting impression. Hailing from the picturesque hills of Dehradun, Uttarakhand, I was born in 1999 with a natural curiosity for both the logical and the creative. My journey through Mathematics and Computer Science has empowered me to blend precision with innovation, creating solutions that live and breathe on the internet.

I specialize in Java, React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and am currently immersing myself in the exciting world of cybersecurity. I’m on a mission to build groundbreaking solutions while continuously evolving in the world of web development.

Beyond the screen, I’ve worn many hats—I'm a proud gold medalist in rifle shooting with the NCC, a passionate photographer and a former member of the photography club at DIT University, and a leader as a former house captain. When I'm not immersed in coding, you’ll find me trekking through the hills, chasing breathtaking views, and capturing moments through my camera lens. The world is my canvas, both digitally and naturally.

Steel Detailing Website

Tech Stack:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Description:

Steel Detailing Website is a responsive website that showcases the services offered by a steel detailing company. The website is designed to be user-friendly and visually appealing, with a clean and modern design. The website features a homepage, services page, projects page, and career page. The homepage includes an about us, our mission, and our vision sections, with a contact form. The services page provides detailed information about the services offered with a scrolling animation. The projects page showcases images of completed projects, with the option to view more details about each project. The career page includes a section showing current openings and a apply form for applicants to get in touch with the company. The website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices.
